About

The game is simple and really fun! Each game speaks instructions and your child can pick the right answer or can choose to explore the sounds of the letters they see on screen.

Every time a letter is tapped the game speaks the sound the letter makes. Instead of teaching kids letter names, which come later, this app teaches the phonetic sound the letter makes, just like they will start to learn in school. The game also encourages children to sound out short words and to start blending sounds together, which is the foundation of reading.
